Pebble Smartwatch App

I like minimal watchfaces on my Pebble Smartwatch. The issue with these is they do not show the date, and the pebble OS didnt have an easy way to just show the date. So I hacked together some code and created an app for the Pebble Appstore that does just that. It shoes the date when you hit one of the buttons on the side of the watch. Various aspects are customizable through your smartphone
